Born Irish. Naturalized Australian. Resident American. Home is where my heart is.
Having graduated B.E. in 2003 from University College Dublin I spent a couple of years in the pharmaceutical industry working on installing instrumentation and digital automation plant before accidentally emigrating to Australia in 2005. The 'lucky country' lived up to its name and a year-long working holiday turned into a career in energy and telecommunications design, engineering and deployment. For over 9 years I worked across the nation-continent on wireless, fiber-optic and emergency service radio networks for Telstra, SingTel Optus, NBNCo and Motorola before once again crossing seas to California.

These days I'm a technical Program Manager at Google Fiber (get ready, #fiberiscoming) managing an awesome team of PgMs and engineers working on deploying new things in new ways. It's a pretty sweet deal :)

The pioneer days pass...the heralds and harbingers of an oncoming civilization, themselves vanish before the civilization for which they have prepared the way.
