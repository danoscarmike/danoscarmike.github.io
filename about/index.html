About me

Born Irish. Naturalized Australian. Resident American. Home is where my heart is.

Having graduated B.E. (Electronic) in 2003 from University College Dublin I spent 2 years working on instrumentation and digital automation before accidentally emigrating to Australia in 2005. A year-long working holiday turned into 9 years in telecommunications design, engineering and deployment. In that time I delivered wireless, fiber-optic and emergency service radio network programs for Telstra, SingTel Optus, NBN and Motorola. In 2014 I once again crossed seas, to California.

These days I'm a Program Manager at Google Fiber (get ready, #fiberiscoming) managing an awesome team of PgMs and engineers working on deploying new things in new ways - willfully attempting to make ourselves redundant. It's a pretty sweet deal :)
