Brexit

For this project I created a visualization of the results of the recent EU referendum in the UK. Using d3.v4 the map of Great Britain and Northern Ireland features drag and zoom, tooltips on mouseover and a statistics panel which transitions into place upon the user clicking an area of interest. The map can be reset by clicking the active local area or anywhere outside the land-mass.
